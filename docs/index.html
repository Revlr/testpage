Yoonsuk Song Composer for Game/Film/Media Bio Yoonsuk Song is a music composer for Game, Film, and Commercial. Yoonsuk has produced many music for various purposes and experienced in sequencing, recording , composing, song writing, and audio production at a various level. He is very passionate in putting strong energy into his music and makes it sound real.

He was born in Seoul, South Korea. He has never quit creating music by being a guitarist since high school. Then, he moved to Orlando to expand his experiences and study Music Production program in Full Sail University.

He spent his time in Los Angeles, worked for many great composers (such as Gerard K. Marino a.k.a composer of God of War games) as an assistant music composer, created music and sound design for many media using programs (Logic Pro, Pro Tools, Cubase, Sibelius, Unity, Wwise, and F Mod Studio).
